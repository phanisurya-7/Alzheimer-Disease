Diet Plan for Alzheimer's Disease

Key Nutrients to Include:

  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Found in fatty fish like salmon, walnuts, and flaxseeds.
  • Antioxidants: Berries, dark leafy greens, and colorful fruits and vegetables.
  • Vitamin E: Nuts, seeds, and leafy greens are good sources.
  • Whole Grains: Quinoa, brown rice, and whole wheat bread provide sustained energy.
  • Lean Proteins: Chicken, turkey, beans, and lentils are excellent choices.

The Alzheimer's Diet:

  • GREEN LEAFY VEGETABLES: Kale, Spinach, Cabbage, Leaf lettuce.
  • NON-STARCHY VEGETABLES: broccoli, cauliflower.
  • FISH: Salmon, Tuna, CatFish.
  • BEANS: Kidney Beans, Black Beans.
  • NUTS: Walnuts, Almonds, Cashews, Hazelnuts.
  • OLIVE OIL
  • BERRIES: Blueberries, Blackberry, Cranberry, Strawberry.
